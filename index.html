GPS-POL, World’s largest archive of political social media data


As the world's largest archive of political social media data, we would like to share our data with other researchers to
hopefully stop the spread of populism in the democratic elections. As we continue to focus on sharing this highly valuable
data, we strive to enrich knoweledge and understanding of how social media in politics can possibly significantly change elections.
