About the Dataset

The GPS-POL dataset is the worldâ€™s largest archive of political social media content, covering candidates, parties, and party alliances across multiple platforms and countries.

  • Millions of posts from political candidates worldwide
  • Multiple election cycles and political systems
  • Standardized party and candidate identifiers

About Us

GPS-POL is led by an interdisciplinary team of scholars in political science, psychology, and computational social science. The project is based at Washington University in St. Louis and collaborates with researchers worldwide.
